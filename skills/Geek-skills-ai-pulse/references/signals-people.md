whose public statements have repeatedly turned into next year's industry
direction, or who is a reliable "reality check" against hype.

Do not expand this list during a run. If the user wants to add or remove
names, that is a separate conversation.

## How to extract from this section

- A person signal is only worth including if the post makes a **specific
  claim**, **announces something**, or **contradicts a widely held view**.
  Daily observations, reposts, and conference photos are not signals.
- Quote the post. Do not paraphrase. The three-field row has a `why it
  matters` field for your interpretation and a `caveat` field for the
  person's known bias — keep them separate from the quote itself.
- If a person has not posted anything non-trivial in the scope window, they
  belong in the `Watchlist (unchanged)` section, not in `People signals`.

## Explicitly out of scope

- Anonymous accounts, regardless of follower count.
- Investor/VC hot-take accounts (noise-to-signal ratio too low for this
  skill's purpose).
- Podcast clips without a primary source — link the primary instead.
